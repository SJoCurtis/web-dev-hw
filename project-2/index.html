How to Make Your Own Cup of Coffee

By: Jo Curtis

“As long as there was coffee in the world, how bad could things be?” - Cassandra Claire, also me on the daily.

 

Okay, so to be honest this is just going to be a web page on how to make a dope cup of coffee for yourself. Most people don't know how to make coffee for themselves, at least decently, and that is what I aim to fix. We all need a good cup of coffee, and whats better? A cup of coffee we made ourselves. 

 

What You Will Need

Just a simple list of supplies before we get into any heavy coffee making content.

  • Chemex 6-cup brewer
  • Square Chemex filter
  • 36 grams (5-7 tbsp) Craft Coffee beans
  • 600 mL water, plus additional for rinsing
  • Pouring Kettle (gooseneck preferred)
  • Timer
  • Drinking Mug
  • Burr grinder (recommended)
  • Scale (recommended)

Also, 

  • Bonus:
    • Total brew time:3:30 mins
    • Yeilds: 2 mugs

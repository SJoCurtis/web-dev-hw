A Litttle Bit About Me

And My Creative Process

Hi! My name is Jo Curtis I am currently a student studying media arts at the University of Montana with a specification in Digital Technology. Besides being scholarly and being a student I actually have a life. I have several hobbies which include:

  • art
  • long-boarding
  • snow-boarding
  • creative coding
  • and of course being an avid coffee drinker. 

 

 I am a cofee addictTM hence why I chose to make this specific instuctable. When faced with this project I was like: what do I care enough about to make an entire freaking webpage on??? Well its either hacking/penetration testing/cybersecurity- which seemed too time consuming and difficult to explain, or my severe love and addiction to coffee. Coffee won, as it always does. It was simple, I love coffee, I want to share my love of coffee with the world, and I want people to know how to make some actually decent coffee. And so here I am, with a real bad webpage about real good coffee. 

 

 

My personal everyday coffee making process is quite different from this webpage, though I do at times indulge myself and make some nice chemex coffee. But usually I just make myself a cup of good ol' fashioned pour over. Mostly out of necessity every morning....afternoon...okay and night. I have a problem. But when I have the time and feel inspired I will go through the long process of making delicious chemex coffee. I usually put on a hygge playlist. Cozy music. Take my sweet time. Let the sweet aroma fill my kitchen. Then I pour it into my favorite black mug. Settle into a nice couch and breath in the coffee fumes. Then I take a soft sip and smile with happiness. My coffee drinking process is normal for the most part, though theres somtimes a passion behind my drinking that is lacking in many others.
